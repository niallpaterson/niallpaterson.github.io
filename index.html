About me.

I'm an academic philosopher and software developer in training. I was born in Auckland, New Zealand, but grew up on the other side of the globe in Gloucestershire, United Kingdom. I'm currently living in and around Bristol.
I have a BA in philosophy from University College London (2013), and a PhD in philosophy from the University of Bristol (2019). My doctoral supervisors were Alexander Bird and Jason Konek. I'm currently changing careers out of academia and into software development. If you want to get in touch, send me an e-mail here.
